What is CR7 favorite car?

CR7 is a big fan of go-fast machines, so you can see why he instantly fell in love with the Ferrari F12 TDF. As with most of Ronaldo's cars, the F12 TDF is rare, with the Italian carmaker building only 799 examples. The F12 TDF cost around $434,998, and it's not even near the most expensive car in his garage.
